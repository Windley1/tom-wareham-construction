About Tom Wareham

I'm Tom Wareham, a builder based in Monmouth with over 12 years of experience in the construction trade. I specialise in house renovations, extensions and new builds across Monmouthshire, Gloucestershire, Newport and the surrounding areas.

As a sole trader, I take personal responsibility for every project. You deal directly with me from the first phone call to the final finish. When a job needs specialist trades, I bring in people I trust and have worked with for years.

I believe in doing things properly — quality materials, solid workmanship, and honest communication throughout. No surprises, no shortcuts.
