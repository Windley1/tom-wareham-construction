Common Questions

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Monmouth and cover Monmouthshire, Gloucestershire, Newport, and the surrounding areas. Not sure if you're in our area? Give us a call and we'll let you know.

Do I need planning permission for an extension or renovation?

It depends on the size and type of work. Many extensions fall under Permitted Development and don't require planning permission. Tom will advise you on what's needed when he comes to quote — and we'll guide you through the process if permission is required.

How do I get a quote?

Fill in the contact form below or call 07379 578 082 directly. Tom will arrange a visit to discuss your project in person and provide a quote — usually within a few days of enquiry.

Are you fully insured?

Yes — Tom Wareham Construction holds full public liability insurance for every job. Happy to share certificate details before work starts on larger projects.

How long have you been in business?

Tom has over 12 years of hands-on construction experience, working across residential renovations, extensions, new builds, stonework and more across Monmouthshire and Gloucestershire.

Do you handle the full project or just part of it?

Tom manages the full build from start to finish. Where a project needs specialist trades — electricians, plumbers, etc. — he brings in trusted people he's worked with for years. You have one point of contact throughout.

Do you work on new builds as well as renovations?

Yes — Tom works across house renovations, extensions, and full new builds. Whatever the scale of the project, the same approach applies: quality materials, solid workmanship, no shortcuts.

Do you provide a written quote before starting?

Yes. After visiting your project, Tom provides a clear written quote so you know exactly what's included and what it will cost before any work begins. No hidden extras.
